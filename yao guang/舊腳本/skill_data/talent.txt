Level 1: Max "Newbud" is based on all characters' levels on the battlefield. For each point of HP lost by all allies, Castorice gains 1 point of "Newbud." When "Newbud" reaches its maximum, Castorice's Ultimate can be used. When allies lose HP, Castorice's and Netherwing's DMG dealt increase by 10%. This effect can stack up to 3 times, lasting for 3 turn(s).
When Netherwing is on the field, "Newbud" cannot be gained, and HP lost by all allies (except Netherwing) will be converted to an equal amount of HP for Netherwing.
