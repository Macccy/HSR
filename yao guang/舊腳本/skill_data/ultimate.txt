Level 1: Summons the memosprite Dead Dragon and advances its action by 100%. At the same time, creates the Territory "Lost Netherland," which decreases all enemies' All-Type RES by 10%. If the DMG Boost effect from Castorice's Talent is active, the effect is extended to Dead Dragon. Dead Dragon has 165 SPD and a set Max HP equal to 100% of max "Stamen Nova."
After Dead Dragon acts 3 times or when its HP is 0, it disappears and dispels the Territory "Lost Netherland."
